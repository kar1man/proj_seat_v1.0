Innovative Design for Smarter Travel

SEAT offers a seamless user interface designed to simplify commuting. With intuitive navigation and smart layouts, users can efficiently access top-ups, real-time train and bus schedules, and personalized notifications.

  • Customizable Designs: Tailored to both web and mobile platforms, SEAT ensures optimal accessibility for everyone.
  • Accessibility Compliance: Fully adheres to WCAG standards, making transportation management accessible to all, including users with disabilities.
  • Responsive Layouts: Adaptable across any device, ensuring a consistent and smooth experience whether on desktop or mobile.
  • Performance Optimization: Lightweight and fast UI components ensure you get the data you need without delays.

SEAT Mobile: All-in-One Transit Solution

SEAT empowers commuters by providing up-to-date and reliable content management tools. Effortlessly manage transit passes, view detailed transaction histories, and stay informed with real-time updates.

  • Pass Management: Easily balance top-ups and monitor your travel expenses.
  • Advisories: Stay informed with real-time public announcements and advisories that matter to your commute.
  • Smart Updates: Transportation updates ensure you’re always one step ahead of delays or route changes.

Download the SEAT Mobile now!

Take SEAT with you wherever you go. Our mobile application ensures real-time access to all features, making it your ultimate transit companion. Available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

  • Real-Time Tracking: Live train and bus schedules to ensure you’re always on time.
  • Convenience on the Go: Top-up and manage your transit card right from your phone.
  • Smart Notifications: Stay updated with location-based notifications, like crowdedness and drop-off stations.

Visit the official SEAT website

Explore SEAT’s features on our user-friendly and responsive website at www.seat.com.ph. Designed to provide all the tools you need to streamline your daily commute.

  • Online Management: Access balance checks, discount applications, and more from the comfort of your home.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for desktops, tablets, and mobile browsers to fit your lifestyle.
  • Comprehensive Info: Find FAQs, train schedules, and system-wide advisories in one place.
